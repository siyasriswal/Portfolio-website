About

I'm currently pursuing my MCA in Artificial Intelligence & Data Science at Graphic Era Hill University, Dehradun — after finishing my BCA there with an 8.28 CGPA. Somewhere between those two degrees I got hooked on the moment a model goes from "guessing" to "knowing": watching a confusion matrix tighten, a loss curve flatten, an F1-score climb past 0.95.

My work so far spans the full classification stack — NLP (fake news, resumes, sentiment), computer vision (crop disease, handwritten digits), and regression/classical ML (house prices, churn, disease risk). I like projects that ship: a Streamlit app, a Flask or FastAPI backend, a React UI someone can actually click around in.

Outside of model training, I spend time on LeetCode sharpening data structures & algorithms, and I'm comfortable across the standard ML/DL toolchain — from preprocessing in Pandas to deploying with Docker.
